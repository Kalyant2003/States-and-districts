* Anantapur district is officially named asAnanthapuramu district.
* It is one of the eight districts in the Rayalaseema region of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.
* The district headquarters is located in Anantapur city.
* It is one of the driest places in South India.
* In the year 2022, as part of re-organisation of districts, Sri Sathya Sai district was carved out.
