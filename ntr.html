Home Back

* Named after former Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao.
* NTR district was proposed on 26 January 2022 and became one of the twenty-six districts in the state after the gazette notification was issued by the Government of Andhra Pradesh on 3 April 2022.
* The gazette notification came into force on 4 April 2022.
* The district was carved out of Krishna district, and consists of the existing Vijayawada revenue division along with Nandigama and Tiruvuru revenue divisions, which were formed along with the district.
