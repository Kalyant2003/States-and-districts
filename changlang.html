* Changlang district is located in the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh, located south of Lohit district and north of Tirap district.
* Naga people reside here . As of 2011 it is the second most populous district of Arunachal Pradesh (out of 16), after Papum Pare.
* It has become one of the major districts in the area owing to the presence of crude oil, coal and mineral resources other than tourism and hydropower
