Home Back

* Kadapa district is officially known as YSR district. formerly: Cuddapah district.
* It is one of the twenty six districts in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.
* In the 2022 reorganisation of Andhra Pradesh districts, the district boundary was largely restricted to the Kadapa parliamentary constituency area.
* It is one of the eight districts in the Rayalaseema region.
* Kadapa is the administrative headquarters for this district.
