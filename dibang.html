* Dibang Valley is a district of Arunachal Pradesh named after the Dibang River or the Talon as the Mishmis call it.
* It is the least populated district in India and has an area of 9,129 square kilometres (3,525 sq mi).In June 1980, Dibang Valley district was created out of part of Lohit district.
* On 16 December 2001, Dibang Valley district was bifurcated into Dibang Valley district and Lower Dibang Valley district.
