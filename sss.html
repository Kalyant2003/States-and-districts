* Sri Sathya Sai district is a district in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.
* Its headquarters is at Puttaparthi.
* It was formed on 4 April 2022 from parts of the erstwhile Anantapur district.
* This district is named after Indian Guru Sri Sathya Sai Baba.
