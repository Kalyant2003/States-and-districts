* Srikakulam district is one of the twenty-six districts of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.
* It is located in the Uttarandhra region of the state, with its headquarters located at Srikakulam.
* It is one of the six districts, located in the extreme northeastern direction of the state.
* It was formerly known as Chicacole, and was under Ganjam district till 1 April 1936, then merged under Vizagapatam district.
* Srikakulam district forms the core area of Kalinga where most of its historical capitals like Kalinganagari, pithunda, Dantapuram are located.
