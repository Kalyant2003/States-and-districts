* Chittoor district is one of the eight districts in the Rayalaseema region of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.
* It had a population of 18,72,951 at the 2011 census of India.
* It is a major market centre for mangoes, grains, sugarcane, and peanuts. The district headquarters is at Chittoor City.
* The major cities/towns in the district are Chittoor, Punganur, Nagari, Palamaner, and Kuppam.
