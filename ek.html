* East Kameng district is one of districts of Arunachal Pradesh state in northeastern, India.
* It shares an international border with China in the north and district borders with West Kameng district to the west, Pakke-Kessang district to the south, Kurung Kumey district to the east, Papum Pare district to the southeast.
* Pakke-Kessang district was bifurcated from East Kameng district on 1 December 2018.
