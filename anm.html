* Annamayya district is one of the eight districts in the Rayalaseema region of Andhra Pradesh, established in 2022.
* Rayachoti is the district administrative headquarters, and Madanapalle is the most populated city in the district.
* The district is named after Annamacharya,a 15th-century Hindu saint that hailed from Tallapaka, Rajampeta.
