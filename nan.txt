<h1 style="text-align:center;">NANDYAL DISTRICT</H1>
<h1 style= "text-align:center;"><img style="width: 50%;" src="Nandyal_in_Andhra_Pradesh_(India).svg.png"><h6>
<p style="font-size:20px;">* Nandyal district is a district in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh with Nandyal as its administrative headquarters.<br>* It was formed on 4 April 2022 to become one of the resultant 26 districts. It is part of the Rayalaseema region.<br>* The district consists of Nandyal revenue division and a newly formed Dhone revenue division and Atmakur revenue division from Kurnool district.</p>
