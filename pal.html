* Palnadu district is a district in coastal Andhra Region in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.
* With Narasaraopet as its administrative headquarters, it was formed on 4 April 2022 to become one of the resultant twenty-six districts.
* The district was formed from Gurazala, Sattenapalli and Narasaraopet revenue divisions from Palnadu district.
* The district covers most of the Palnadu region.
